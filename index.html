Our mission

Back in grade 10, I launched my own cookie business driven by my passion for baking and a desire to make a positive impact during the Covid pandemic. Every Sunday for two years, I dedicated myself to baking over 100 cookies, pouring love and creativity into each batch. Through this endeavor, I successfully raised over 1,500 Canadian dollars for the mental health organization, Jack.org. It was incredibly fulfilling to see my hobby transform into a meaningful venture that contributed to a cause I deeply care about. As a first year university student I had to press pause on my business as I do not have an oven but am hopeful that I will be able to restart it next year. I am eager to continue pursuing my passion for baking and community service, and I look forward to the opportunity to once again make a positive impact through my cookie business.

Who we are

Hi there! My name is Kassandra Lee and along with having a passion of baking, I have a strong desire to give back to my community. Mental health is something that is close to my heart and giving back to my community is something I cherish. Growing up going to an international school and playing hockey sheltered me for most of my life, something which I am extremely grateful for, but unfortunately is not a accurate representation of the world. This project was the birthchild of a passion and my alturistic character. Hope you guys enjoy!

Jack.org, headquartered in Toronto, is a Canadian mental health organization focusing on addressing mental health challenges among teenagers and young adults. It stands out for its approach of engaging young leaders to advocate for mental health awareness and combat stigma associated with it. By empowering and training these emerging leaders, Jack.org endeavors to spearhead a transformative movement in mental health across Canada.
